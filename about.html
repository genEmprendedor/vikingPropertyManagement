About

Viking Property Management Ltd.

Viking Property Management Ltd. (Viking) was formed with a specific focus on providing a positive maintenance experience for clients (i.e., boards of directors, maintenance committees, landlords, etc.) that is enjoyable for your members / tenants / owners.

Viking ensures projects are completed on budget and on time, along with providing a positive experience by:

  • By organizing and completing all maintenance projects using standard project management systems;
  • Routine site visits with contractor and members / tenants / owners to ensure members are pleased with the work;
  • Regular surveys of clients, members, and tenants about Viking’s and our contractor’s performance; and
  • Using a “one window” approach to communication between the client and the contractor for each and every project.

Quinn Bottorff

Project Manager & President

Quinn Bottorff is Viking’s Project Manager and Viking’s President. He oversees Viking operations, and all maintenance work and construction management projects.


Quinn has over 20 years of experience managing a wide variety of projects (construction, excavations, foundations, reclamation, environmental assessments, etc.) while working with a number of engineering / environmental consulting firms and property management firms; and the provincial and federal governments. In particular, Quinn has a Bachelor’s degree and Master’s degree in the ecological sciences and as result is a Professional Agrologist with a specialty in Management.


While going through university Quinn was involved in a variety of construction projects ranging from installing sidewalks, framing residential houses, fence installations, bathroom and basement renovations, and residential roofing. These experiences have provided him with experience in long term planning, management, construction, quality control/ assurance, and contractor management.


To add to his project management skills Quinn is also currently completing his Project Management Professional (PMP) Certification through the Project Management Institute and NAIT.
